# DESIGN.md

## Design intent
Quiet, minimal, and readable. The site should feel like a neutral procedure viewer, not a flashy game UI.

## Rules
- Do not over-decorate backgrounds.
- Keep images central and large.
- Use short text blocks.
- Avoid revealing pictogram meanings through labels.
- Use `A`, `B`, `C` style step labels in the public UI.

## Accessibility
- High contrast text.
- Large tap targets.
- Works on iPhone Safari.
- No auto-advancing.
